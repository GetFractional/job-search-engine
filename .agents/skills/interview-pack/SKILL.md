# Interview Pack Skill

## Purpose
Prepare role-specific interview strategy, likely questions, answer drafts, stories, objections, rebuttals, questions to ask, and follow-up messaging.

## Required Sources
1. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/06_interview_pack_template.md`

## Inputs
- Research brief
- Fit scorecard
- Tailored resume
- Interview stage
- Interviewers
- Known concerns

## Process
1. Summarize role, company, and interview stage.
2. Diagnose why the role exists.
3. Choose 2 to 4 best stories.
4. Draft 60-second and 2-minute "Tell me about yourself" answers.
5. Prepare likely questions, objections, rebuttals, and questions to ask.
6. Draft follow-up message.

## Output
- Interview pack
- Story map
- Objection handling
- Follow-up draft

## Safety
Every answer must preserve claim safety and avoid unsupported ownership.
