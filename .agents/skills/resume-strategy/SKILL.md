# Resume Strategy Skill

## Purpose
Create a role-specific resume strategy before drafting.

## Required Sources
1. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/01_matt_dimock_canonical_profile.md`
2. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/02_metrics_ledger.md`
3. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/03_role_lane_glossary.md`
4. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/04_story_bank.md`
5. `templates/tailored-resume-brief.md`

## Inputs
- Role intake
- Full JD
- Research brief
- Fit scorecard
- Teal Job Matcher notes, if available

## Process
1. Confirm role lane and mandate.
2. Select the target title and 2-line summary angle.
3. Select the strongest safe metrics and proof stories.
4. Identify truthful JD keywords.
5. Identify unsupported keywords to avoid.
6. Plan a two-page reverse-chronological resume.

## Output
- Resume strategy brief
- Proof hierarchy
- Keyword strategy
- Claim safety table
- Drafting instructions

## Safety
Do not draft from narrative strategy alone. Strategy must cite source-backed proof.
