# Hiring Manager And Recruiter Research Skill

## Purpose
Identify relevant people, likely priorities, credibility triggers, and the safest outreach angle.

## Required Sources
1. `docs/teal-workflow.md`
2. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/05_outreach_templates.md`
3. `templates/linkedin-outreach.md`
4. `templates/recruiter-email.md`
5. `templates/hiring-manager-email.md`

## Inputs
- Company
- Role
- Recruiter name, if known
- Hiring manager name, if known
- Functional leader, if known
- Job description

## Process
1. Identify likely recruiter, hiring manager, functional leader, and relevant executives.
2. Infer each person's likely priority from role, title, and company context.
3. Identify safe credibility triggers from Matt's proof stack.
4. Draft outreach only after research and fit score justify it.
5. Keep messages short, specific, calm, and easy to answer.

## Output
- People map
- Likely priorities
- Outreach angle
- Draft message options
- Approval checklist

## Safety
Do not scrape or bypass platform rules. Do not send messages without approval.
