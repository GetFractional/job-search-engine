# Compensation And Offer Strategy Skill

## Purpose
Preserve leverage, avoid sounding desperate, negotiate toward the best possible package, and handle low offers professionally.

## Required Sources
1. `docs/role-fit-scorecard.md`
2. `templates/compensation-negotiation-prep.md`
3. Role research and market compensation data

## Inputs
- Company
- Role
- Stage
- Posted range
- Recruiter statements
- Offer details
- Benefits details
- Competing opportunities

## Process
1. Assess base, bonus, equity, benefits, 401(k), healthcare, childcare, relocation, and remote value.
2. Compare against ideal target, preferred minimum, and strategic floor.
3. Identify leverage and risks.
4. Recommend anchor, minimum, concessions, and non-cash asks.
5. Draft calm negotiation language.

## Output
- Compensation fit analysis
- Negotiation strategy
- Draft language
- Walk-away conditions

## Safety
Do not negotiate externally without approval. Avoid premature low anchors.
