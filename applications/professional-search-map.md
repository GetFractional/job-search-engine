# Professional Search Map

## Time-box
- `45-60 minutes` each weekday morning after hospitality applications
- no same-day professional work during the Broadway walk-in window

## Lane 1: Revenue Operations / GTM Operations
### Target titles
- Director of Revenue Operations
- Senior Manager, Revenue Operations
- GTM Operations Lead
- Director of GTM Operations
- Revenue Enablement + Systems roles with cross-functional scope

### Must-have mandate signals
- CRM / lifecycle / reporting ownership
- cross-functional funnel definitions and handoffs
- enablement, onboarding, or process design
- dashboards, instrumentation, or operations visibility

### Hard pass signals
- pure sales-ops admin
- reporting-only analyst roles
- degree-gated ops roles with no practical flexibility

## Lane 2: Lifecycle / CRM / Retention
### Target titles
- Director of Lifecycle Marketing
- Director of CRM
- Director of Retention
- Lifecycle and Retention Lead

### Must-have mandate signals
- retention or engagement ownership
- segmentation and automation
- cross-functional customer-journey ownership
- reporting tied to repeat revenue or behavior

### Hard pass signals
- email-production-heavy specialist jobs
- campaign execution with no systems ownership

## Lane 3: Growth / Revenue Marketing
### Target titles
- Director of Growth Marketing
- Director of Revenue Marketing
- Head of Growth
- Director of Demand Generation when it is systems-heavy

### Must-have mandate signals
- experimentation and CRO
- reporting and revenue accountability
- lifecycle coordination
- multi-channel operating leadership

### Hard pass signals
- paid-media-only
- brand-heavy
- SDR-pipeline-only SaaS demand gen with no broader systems work

## Lane 4: Ecommerce Growth / CRO / Retention
### Target titles
- Director of Ecommerce Growth
- Director of CRO and Retention
- Director of Ecommerce and Retention
- Head of Ecommerce Growth

### Must-have mandate signals
- owned-channel growth
- CRO and site experience
- lifecycle or retention ownership
- reporting tied to sessions, conversion, and repeat purchase

### Hard pass signals
- merchandising-heavy
- inventory planning or retail-buying-heavy
- marketplace-only growth with no customer-journey layer
