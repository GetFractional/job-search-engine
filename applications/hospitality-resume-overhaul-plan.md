# Hospitality Resume Overhaul Plan

## Objective
Rebuild the hospitality resumes so they:
- keep the recent timeline intact
- use earlier retail and management work as direct credibility
- avoid corporate jargon
- still show that Matt has been working recently and seriously

## What went wrong
The previous drafts overcorrected.

They solved the tone problem by creating a chronology problem:
- too much recent experience was removed
- the page started to imply a `2007-2019` gap
- the remaining recent bullets were still too abstract
- the overall read became less believable, not more

## Correct strategy
Do not hide recent experience.

Compress and translate it.

### Keep recent experience on the page
Use `3 recent entries`, with `1 bullet each`, focused on:
- pace
- follow-through
- communication
- training
- checklists
- organization
- handling multiple priorities under pressure

Recommended recent entries to keep available:
1. `Get Fractional`
2. `Prosper Wireless`
3. `Bob's Watches`, `Breakthrough Academy`, or `OC Ramps`, depending on lane

### Keep earlier retail and management work visible
Use `Lowe's Home Improvement` and `Boething Treeland Nursery` as direct support-floor credibility.

These prove:
- customer service
- stocking
- physical work
- department ownership
- opening / closing responsibility
- team leadership
- product recall
- working around customers while staying organized

## Recommended section order
1. `Name + Contact`
2. `Target Title`
3. `3-line Summary`
4. `Core Strengths`
5. `Recent Experience`
6. `Earlier Retail & Team Leadership Experience`
7. `Additional Information`
