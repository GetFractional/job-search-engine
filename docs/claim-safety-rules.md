# Claim Safety Rules

## Core Rule
Work from evidence, not invention. Never invent metrics, ownership, tools, titles, or outcomes.

## Source Check
Before using any number:
1. Check `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/02_metrics_ledger.md`.
2. Confirm confidence: High, Medium, Low, or Unverified.
3. Confirm ownership: Direct, Shared leadership, Supporting, or Context only.
4. Use the listed safe phrasing or a more conservative version.
5. Avoid over-attribution.

## Claim Labels
Use these labels during analysis:
- Validated: Supported by source and safe to use with correct phrasing.
- Estimated: Directionally useful, must use approximating language.
- Inferred: Reasonable internal interpretation, do not present as fact externally.
- Weak: Needs better support before external use.
- Missing: Not available in the source set.
- Risky: Could overstate ownership, precision, or relevance.
- Do not use externally: Keep out of resumes, cover letters, outreach, LinkedIn, and applications.

## Safe Verbs
Prefer:
- helped grow
- supported scale
- contributed to
- built systems that enabled
- architected infrastructure behind
- improved
- increased
- reduced
- established
- implemented

Avoid unless clearly validated:
- single-handedly drove
- owned all growth
- directly generated all revenue
- transformed the company
- built from nothing
- dominated

## Prohibited Or Restricted Claims
Do not use externally unless later validated and added to the Metrics Ledger:
- `$170M` aggregate revenue growth
- precise CAC claims
- exact churn or LTV claims
- unsupported paid-media ROAS
- exaggerated AI claims
- claims that imply Matt alone drove sales-led company growth
- reference contact information

## AI Claims
AI is a meaningful differentiator, not the headline.

Safe framing:
- designed AI-assisted workflows
- used AI to reduce manual work and improve decision quality
- partnered with engineering to operationalize AI processes
- built workflow architecture involving AI tools

Avoid:
- AI wizard
- AI-native visionary
- fully autonomous recruiting AI unless validated
- anything that sounds theatrical or inflated

## External Asset QA
Before any asset is used externally:
- every number must trace to the Metrics Ledger
- every major story must trace to the Story Bank or Canonical Profile
- every role lane must trace to the Role Lane Glossary
- every reference use must be approved
- every compensation statement must preserve leverage
- every message must sound human, calm, specific, and credible
