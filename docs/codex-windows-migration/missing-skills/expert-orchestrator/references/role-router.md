# Role Router

Use roles as decision lenses. Do not roleplay them.

## Default Rules

- Choose 1 lead role and 1 to 3 support roles.
- Expand to 4 to 10 support lenses only when the task is broad, cross-functional, multi-stage, high-stakes, or the user explicitly asks for a wider panel.
- Match roles to the bottleneck, not to an org chart.
- Every added support role must cover a distinct bottleneck, decision, stage, or risk.
- If support lenses exceed 3, rank them and separate `active now` from `reference if needed`.
- Add risk or QA when the work is external, irreversible, or trust-sensitive.
- If the user asked for execution, include at least one builder role.

## Core Roles

- Strategist: market choice, wedge, tradeoffs, competitive posture.
- Product lead: JTBD, scope, user flow, requirements, success metrics.
- Growth lead: acquisition, activation, retention, monetization, experiments.
- UX lead: navigation, interaction, hierarchy, friction removal, heuristics.
- Copy lead: positioning, messaging, clarity, CTA logic, objections.
- Research lead: source quality, evidence, user insight, unknown mapping.
- Solutions architect: system design, technical feasibility, integration risk.
- Engineer: implementation path, complexity, edge cases, testability.
- Analyst: economics, measurement, cohorts, experiment readouts.
- Operations lead: sequencing, SOPs, dependencies, critical path.
- Risk lead: privacy, security, compliance, reputation, failure guards.
- Creative lead: concepting, story, format, production system, polish.

## Common Working Groups

- New offer or GTM:
  - Lead: strategist
  - Support: growth, copy, product
- Pricing or packaging:
  - Lead: strategist
  - Support: analyst, growth, copy
- UX friction or onboarding:
  - Lead: product
  - Support: UX, copy, analyst
- 0 to 1 validation:
  - Lead: product
  - Support: research, growth, analyst
- Complex software delivery:
  - Lead: solutions architect
  - Support: engineer, product, risk
- High-stakes external launch:
  - Lead: strategist
  - Support: product, copy, operations, risk
- Content or creative engine:
  - Lead: growth
  - Support: copy, creative, analyst
- Video or ad creative:
  - Lead: creative
  - Support: growth, copy

## Selection Questions

Ask:
- What is the main bottleneck: demand, clarity, conversion, fulfillment, systems, or risk?
- What decision must be made next?
- What proof is missing?
- Which role would most reduce rework right now?
