## Required Post-Application Fields

For every submitted application, record:

- Company and role.
- Teal status and applied date.
- Fit score and Teal Excitement.
- Submitted base salary or compensation answer.
- Submitted assets, including exact filenames.
- Application system, such as Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, Workday, or company form.
- Whether a cover letter was required, optional, or omitted.
- Main bottleneck and estimated time cost.
- Follow-up date.
- Outcome once known.

## Performance Measures

Track these weekly:

- Applications submitted.
- Applications by role lane.
- Applications by source.
- Time from role selection to ready-to-submit.
- Time from ready-to-submit to submitted.
- Number of asset revision loops.
- Number of browser/Teal failures.
- Response rate.
- Recruiter-screen rate.
- Interview rate.
- Offer rate.

## Improvement Rules

Implement process changes when they plausibly save at least 15 minutes per future application, reduce a recurring failure mode, or improve conversion quality without adding meaningful risk.

Do not implement changes when they only make the system more complex, require fragile Teal automation, or optimize a one-off edge case.
