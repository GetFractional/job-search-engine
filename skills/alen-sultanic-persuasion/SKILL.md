# Alen Sultanic Persuasion Skill

## Purpose
Use Alen Sultanic-style persuasion internally as a strategic lens for clarity, proof, positioning, and conversion. Do not name-drop this externally.

## Required Sources
1. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/05_outreach_templates.md`
2. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/01_matt_dimock_canonical_profile.md`

4. Role research and JD

## Inputs
- Asset or message
- Audience
- Role mandate
- Company pain
- Proof points

## Process
1. Identify the core pain or buying trigger.
2. Identify the most believable proof.
3. Strip vague claims and clever-but-empty language.
4. Make the angle sharper, simpler, and more commercially specific.
5. Keep the final output human, calm, grounded, and credible.

## Output
- Sharpened positioning angle
- Proof hierarchy
- Removed language
- Revised draft or strategic notes

## Safety
Do not produce hype, theatrical phrasing, manipulation, false urgency, or gimmicky branding. Do not mention this lens externally.
