# Market And Competition Skill

## Purpose
Understand market pressure, competitor positioning, category trends, and what the company likely needs.

## Required Sources
1. `templates/company-research-brief.md`
2. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/03_role_lane_glossary.md`
3. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/04_story_bank.md`

## Inputs
- Company
- Category
- Role
- JD
- Known competitors

## Process
1. Identify direct and adjacent competitors.
2. Summarize category dynamics and demand signals.
3. Identify market pressure that could make the role urgent.
4. Translate market pressure into likely internal operating needs.
5. Connect those needs to Matt's safest proof themes.

## Output
- Competitor map
- Market pressure summary
- Why the role may exist now
- Strategic positioning angle
- Risks and unknowns

## Safety
Use current sources when market facts can change. Label assumptions as inference.
