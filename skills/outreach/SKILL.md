# Outreach Skill

## Purpose
Create LinkedIn connection notes, LinkedIn follow-ups, recruiter emails, hiring manager emails, and warm-intro blurbs.

## Required Sources
1. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/05_outreach_templates.md`
2. `/Users/mattdimock/Downloads/02_metrics_ledger.md`
3. `templates/linkedin-outreach.md`
4. `templates/recruiter-email.md`
5. `templates/hiring-manager-email.md`

## Inputs
- Research brief
- Fit scorecard
- Contact/persona
- Role lane
- Proof themes
- Desired next step

## Process
1. Decide whether the message is for recruiter, hiring manager, executive, employee, or warm intro.
2. Use a specific reason for outreach.
3. Use 1 to 3 safe proof points max.
4. Keep it short, calm, specific, and easy to answer.
5. Prepare QA and approval checklist.

## Output
- Message options
- Recommended send order
- Follow-up timing
- Approval checklist

## Safety
Do not send without approval. Do not over-explain or dump resume content.
