# Expert Team Orchestration Skill

## Purpose
For each major job-search task, think like a coordinated expert team while producing one clear output.

## Expert Lenses
- Executive recruiter
- ATS resume architect
- Compensation strategist
- GTM / RevOps strategist
- Ecommerce growth strategist
- Lifecycle / CRM strategist
- Company research analyst
- Market intelligence analyst
- Hiring manager psychologist
- Executive communications strategist
- Interview coach
- AI workflow architect

## Inputs
- User task
- Role intake
- JD
- Research brief
- Fit scorecard
- Asset draft, if applicable

## Process
1. Identify which expert lenses matter for the task.
2. Use recruiter and ATS lenses to keep fit obvious and parseable.
3. Use strategy lenses to diagnose the business problem.
4. Use psychology and communications lenses to reduce friction and improve clarity.
5. Use compensation lens when comp, offer, or leverage is involved.
6. Use QA lens before anything external.

## Output
- Coordinated recommendation
- Key risks
- Best next action
- Draft asset or decision, if requested

## Safety
This is an internal reasoning framework. Do not create verbose "expert team" theatrics in external assets.
